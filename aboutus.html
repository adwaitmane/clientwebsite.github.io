About Us

The aim of this project was simple. Deploy from almost any beach, get the line out as far and as fast as possible, precisely target successful known locations, and catch as much as you need. The next time, do it exactly the same way again, or alternatively, modify the bait drop to suit conditions. The approach was clear. Talk to fishing experts, learn what was required, and use the best and most reliable technologies to build the best fishing machines available. Think outside the box, and hey, even make it fly if it helped… and so we did. In March 2014, the world’s first AeroKontiki fishing drone was born.
